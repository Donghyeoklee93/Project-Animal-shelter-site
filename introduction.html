Dogs are our link to paradise


On Dec 01, 2020, Animal shelter was established

Animal Shelter embraces, above all, kindness, respect, compassion,

awareness and integrity in all aspects of our mission and vision for a cruelty-free world.

“Saving one dog will not change the world, but surely for that one dog, the world will change forever.”



Vision


Our vision is for all animals to live a life free of cruelty and suffering.

A humane and compassionate future for every animal friend.

We are thoughtful, passionate, responsive, and steadfast caregivers

for animal friends in our care and community.


Mission


To rescue, rehabilitate and rehome animals in crisis,

ensure healthy pets through education,

advocacy and affordable services, and inspire a community

where the animal-human bond is celebrated and nurtured.
